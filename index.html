ABOUT STREET WORKOUT
Street Workout is a fun and interesting exercise trend that combines the muscle-building power of calisthenics with the endurance-building power of athletics. Instead of going to the gym to lift heavy weights, Street Workout works on the principle of body weight training, using your own weight for resistance.
